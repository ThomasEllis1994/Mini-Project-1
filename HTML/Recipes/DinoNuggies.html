Dinosaur Chicken Nuggets

Time:

16 minutes

Ingredients

  • 8 Nuggets of Questionable Meat

Dino Nuggies

Instructions



1.]Preheat oven to 350F. Then insert the dino nuggies into the oven. After 8 minutes, flip them. Cook for 8 more minutes, then remove from oven and serve.
